# Product vision

AI Startup Builder helps founders turn a raw idea into an actionable launch brief. The first feature is idea analysis: a user submits an idea and receives a structured business outline they can refine into a plan, architecture, UI, and code.

## MVP boundaries

- No authentication, persistent database, wallet, or payment flow yet.
- No external AI request or secret is needed.
- Generated analysis is guidance, not market research or financial advice.
